8Crafter's .mcstructure File Importer for the mobile versions of Minecraft Bedrock Edition v1.1.0

Instructions

  • Click on the "Import .mcstructure files" button at the bottom of the screen.
  • Upload the .mcstructure files that you want to import into Minecraft.
  • Optionally adjust the structure names for any of the structures, these will be what you put into the structure block in-game.
  • Click the generate button at the bottom of the screen.
  • Add the generated pack to your Minecraft world.
  • Load in the structures in a structure block using their structure names. If you forget the structure names, you can always look at the pack description where it lists them.
